ABOUT THE WORKSHOP

It's been more than a year since the launch of PRAYOG, an initiative by CTARA, IIT Bombay headed by Prof. Bakul Rao which works towards solving the developmental problems in society through structured research while leveraging the funding through CSR and the government. PAYOG has been working towards bringing the stakeholders like solution seekers and solution providers together.
PRAYOG is planning to conduct a 2-day workshop in the second week of July 2023 to bring CSRs and NGOs together. We are expecting an enormous response to this workshop. We have prepared a questionnaire to shortlist the NGOs who can present their projects in front of CSRs and stand a chance to get funding. Apart from this, NGOs will also be trained for identifying and defining problems and writing impactive proposals for enhancing the chances to raise funds.
Corporates with a CSR mandate will explore potential funding opportunities, and learn from experts in CSR policy and compliance. Companies willing to fund projects can connect with NGOs working on socially and environmentally impactful initiatives.
